About

I design and build AI‑driven visual tools and immersive experiences that help non‑AI experts understand and act on complex data. My work sits at the intersection of HCI, UX research and data science — delivering production‑ready visualisations, prototypes and evaluation workflows. I have experience leading cross‑disciplinary projects, writing grant proposals, and shipping customer‑facing web tools.

Selected roles include Data Visualiser at Intelligent Growth Solutions (Plotly.js + Angular), Tech/Research Fellow at Glasgow Caledonian University/CTI, UX freelance work and Product Design Engineering in R&D. I hold a PhD in Data Visualisation (GCU).
